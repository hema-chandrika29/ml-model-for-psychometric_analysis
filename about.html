    This website is build by the team Cosmic stars
    The website streamlines candidate assessment with two steps: answering questions for scoring and providing social media links. An advanced ML model analyzes the candidate's social media content, including posts, comments, and followed hashtags, to predict their personality type. Initially focused on sales and tech roles, the system matches candidates to roles based on their personality assessments. Its scalable design allows it to adapt to various roles, serving the organization's specific requirements, thus optimizing the hiring process by efficiently aligning candidates with job positions. This comprehensive approach enhances the candidate-organization fit and ensures more successful, sustainable hires while also increasing operational efficiency.
